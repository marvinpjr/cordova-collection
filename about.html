Superior Market Location

The 53, 925 square Foot, 5 building Cordova Collection at Dexter is a premier retail property at one of the most desirable intersections within the highest income demographic in the Memphis, Tennessee Metropolitan Area. The property is situated on the northwest corner of six lane North Germantown Parkway (43,657 ADT) and 6 lane Dexter Road (16,403 ADT).

The subject Cordova Collection at Dexter is situated within an intensely developed retail corridor along Germantown Parkway in the upper and middle upper income area known as Wolfchase in suburban east Memphis, Tennessee. Approximately 1 mile north of the subject property is the Wolfchase Mall, a 1.3 million sf enclosed mall owned by Simon Malls, anchored by Macy’s, Dillard’s, JC Penny’s and Sears.

Directly across Dexter Road from the Cordova Collection at Dexter is the 220,496 sf Commons at Dexter lake shopping center, anchored by Kroger, Steinmart, HomeGoods, Marshall’s, Tuesday Morning and Dressbarn. Within 1 1⁄2 miles north and south of the subject property along Germantown Road are a number of national retail tenants freestanding and in shopping centers including: IKEA, Dick’s. Ashley Furniture, Costco, Home Depot, Target, Lowe’s Walmart, Petco and Party City.
